POSTURA

Welcome to POSTURA — a tool to help you measure Cervical Vertebral Angle (CVA).

Instructions

1. Upload a clear side-view photo or use your camera.
2. Click on the tragus (ear) first.
3. Then click on C7 (neck base).
4. The system calculates your CVA angle for up to 3 attempts.
